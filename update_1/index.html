Rachel’s experience in communication and publishing, as an editor and writer for various magazines and independent fashion brands, includes Unseen Photo Fair’s inaugural magazine and VICE's ad agency in Amsterdam.

Rachel currently splits her time between freelance communication work for artists, photography publishers, cultural institutes and galleries and her photo-based work.

After completing an MA in Documentary Photography from LCC (University of Arts London), Rachel explores mostly analogue photography, along with archive and found imagery and materials for personal projects and collaborations.

Her interest lies in the materiality of photography and the photo as a cultural object and artifact. She collects photobooks, photography zines and #believesinfilm.
